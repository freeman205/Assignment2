Frequently Asked Questions

What is AdChain Pro?

AdChain Pro is a premium ad viewing platform that connects users with high-quality advertisements from top brands worldwide. Our main goal is to create a rewarding experience where users can earn points by viewing relevant ads, which can then be redeemed for cash, gift cards, and other valuable rewards. We make advertising beneficial for everyone involved.

What types of advertisements do you show?

We partner with premium brands and advertisers to show high-quality video ads, interactive content, surveys, and promotional materials. All advertisements are carefully curated to ensure they're relevant, engaging, and from reputable companies. We focus on quality over quantity to provide the best user experience.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on your selected plan, viewing activity, and engagement level. Our basic plan starts at just $15 and can generate daily rewards. Premium plans offer higher earning potential with exclusive ad content and bonus multipliers. Most active users earn between $50-$200 per month.

How do I get paid?

We offer multiple payout options including PayPal, bank transfer, gift cards from popular retailers, and digital wallet transfers. Payments are processed daily for active users, and you can request withdrawals once you reach the minimum threshold of $10.

Is AdChain Pro free to join?

Yes! Creating an account is completely free, and new users receive a $25 welcome bonus. While we offer premium plans for enhanced earning potential, you can start earning immediately with our free tier. There are no hidden fees or charges.

How do I maximize my earnings?

To maximize earnings: 1) Complete your profile for better ad targeting, 2) View ads consistently every day, 3) Engage with interactive content, 4) Refer friends for bonus commissions, 5) Upgrade to premium plans for higher rates, and 6) participate in special campaigns and surveys.
